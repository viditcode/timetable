Weekdays = 2 hrs total (1 hr tech + 1 hr project, or 1 hr finance on alternate days). Weekends = 3–4 hrs total. Exercise 4×/week — Mon, Wed, Fri + Sat. Finance 3×/week — Tue, Thu, Fri.
This is a flexible template — shift slots by ±1 hour to fit your life. Missing one day is fine. Missing the whole week is a signal to simplify, not quit.

Phase 1 — Learn (months 1–4)

Foundation mode

Your only goal is skill-building and building one small project to completion. No income pressure yet.

Tech focus
QA automation basics → API testing → 1 AI tool integration → small portfolio project
Finance focus
Personal budget audit → index funds basics → SIP setup (even ₹500/month) → compound interest math

Phase 2 — Earn (months 4–12)

Income mode

Shift 60% of side-project time to client work or monetization. First ₹5K–₹20K/month is the milestone.

Tech focus
Freelance QA / backend gigs → build & ship 1 paid tool → contribute to open source for visibility
Finance focus
Invest 20–30% of side income → study equity / mutual funds → build 3-month emergency fund

Phase 3 — Scale (year 1+)

Scale mode

Double down on what's working. Automate, delegate, or productize. ₹1L+/month side income → ₹1 crore in 8–12 years with consistent investing.

Tech focus
SaaS micro-product → AI consulting → build audience / newsletter → hire or collaborate
Finance focus
Portfolio rebalancing → tax-efficient investing → ELSS + equity → start tracking net worth quarterly
When you hit a milestone, upgrade your timetable — not before. Premature scaling causes burnout.

Milestone upgrade map

1
First project completed & live
Upgrade: Add 30 min of marketing / outreach on Saturday. Start building in public (LinkedIn, Twitter). Reduce pure learning from 2 hrs/day to 1.5 hrs and add 30 min networking.
2
First paid income (₹1K–₹5K)
Upgrade: Treat side income like a real business — open a separate account. Replace 1 evening tech session with client work. Add monthly income tracking to Sunday review.
3
Consistent ₹20K/month side income
Upgrade: Invest minimum 25% of all side income automatically. Begin productizing a repeatable skill. Evaluate if daytime job energy is a bottleneck — consider transitions.
4
₹50K–₹1L/month side income
Upgrade: Hire a VA or junior collaborator for routine tasks. Shift schedule to 80% scaling, 20% learning. Consult a SEBI-registered advisor. Model your ₹1 crore timeline.
5
Net worth ₹10L+ invested
Upgrade: Rebalance portfolio (equity + debt mix). Begin thinking about passive income (dividend ETFs, REITs). Your timetable at this point should be 50% product/business, 30% finance, 20% learning.

The one rule that makes this work

Consistency beats intensity. 90 minutes of focused work 5 days/week beats a heroic 8-hour weekend session. If a week collapses, return Monday with zero guilt. The system resets — you don't lose progress by pausing, only by quitting.
